Employee Payroll Management

It is an Internet-based Java app that automates the working of a company.Workers may access their details, schedules, and attendance while the supervisor manages numerous tasks including maintaining salary data, tracking attendance, and updating employee details.

Tools used: Eclipse,Spring Boot,MySql,Angular,Postman, Selenium.

mAadhar Application

An online application for the process of applying an Aadhar Card by making it smoother for Indian citizens which has two major divisions for admin and citizen logins .This Application is created to register citizens and let the administrative authorities to display their details to process their Aadhar Card application.

Tools used: Eclipse,Angular

FlyAway Application

Flyaway is an online flight ticket booking portal. One can easily search flights and book accordingly to their travel.Passengers can book their flights by user login and all the details are stored in the backend.

Tools used: Eclipse,JDBC,JSP,MySql
